Features

Everything you need to stay on top of your child's class notes.

📷

Photo Notes

Share multiple photos per note. Images are automatically optimized for fast loading.

📚

Organized by Subject

Filter notes by Math, Science, English, Hindi, Social Studies — or add your own custom subjects.

🔔

Request Notes

Ask the whole group or a specific parent for notes your child missed.

🔒

Private Groups

Groups are invite-only. Share the code only with parents you trust.

🔔

Push Notifications

Get notified instantly when someone shares notes or responds to your request.

🌐

Cross-Platform

Works on iPhone, iPad, and Android. Everyone in the group stays connected.
