## Full Description (Both Stores)

ClassNotes makes it easy for parents and teachers to share class notes, homework pages, and study materials within their child's school group.

**Never miss a day of school notes again.**

When your child is absent, sick, or forgets to copy their notes â€” just open ClassNotes and ask the group. Other parents can snap a photo and share it instantly.

**How it works:**

1. Create or join your class group using a simple invite code
2. Share notes by taking a photo and selecting the subject
3. Request notes when your child misses class
4. Get notified when someone shares what you need

**Key Features:**

- Share Notes: Photograph and share class notes organized by subject and date
- Request Notes: Ask the group (or a specific parent) when you need notes
- Subject Filters: Quickly find Math, Science, English, Hindi, Social Studies, or custom subjects
- Group Management: Create groups for each class, invite parents with a code
- Photo Viewer: Pinch-to-zoom on shared note photos
- Push Notifications: Get alerted when notes you requested are shared
- Comments & Reactions: Engage with shared notes
- Privacy Focused: Phone number sign-in, no personal data collected

**Built for Indian schools. Works for any school worldwide.**

ClassNotes is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.
